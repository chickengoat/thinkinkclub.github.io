I am hungry now\
want to eat vatana\
salty tangy snack pack\
tummy full I'm back

am slave to tongue\
my craving bowel rung\
cravings food far flung\
no control man young


step by step today\
tomorrow day after day\
will tame my bite\
and discipline my apetite
